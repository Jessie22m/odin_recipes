Fried Chicken

The Best Fried Chicken, made from Scratch!

Here we feature amazing crispy breaded fried chicken with our signature special recipe

    Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Breading
  • Chili Powder, Bread Crumbs, Salt, Pepper

    Steps

  1. Combine the salt, pepper, and chili Powder
  2. Add it to the bread crumbs
  3. Roll the chicken into the crumbs and peppers, and fry it!
