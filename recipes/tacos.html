Taco's

The Best Tacos, made from the best beef!

This right here features the best beef and cheddar tacos you will ever taste in your life, you can also use chicken if you please.

    Ingredients

  • Taco Beef
  • Seasoning
  • Queso

    Steps

  1. Mix the beef with the seasoning
  2. Add queso to the beef
  3. Put in a taco shell with whatever you like
